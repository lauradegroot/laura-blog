october 4th, 2012

Doodle on wood

I had the lovely experience of getting some wood cut at Home Depot for a final project, and decided to make something with the leftovers. The problem with making heavy wall art is that my impending move is going to be a massive hassle.
I’ve always dreamed of a house with walls covered in picture frames, and got a problematic head start early on in life. It’s doubly problematic seeing as I’ve moved 9 times in the last 5 years (wow, that number hurts my brain and makes my box-lifting muscles twinge). From now until forever I’ll be lugging these dang pictures around. I made another one! Yay!
